About Us

We Are One Of The World's Best University

We are a vibrant and forward-thinking university dedicated to making a positive impact on individuals and communities through education, research, and innovation. Established in 1989, AUT has expanded significantly in both enrollment and campus infrastructure, now operating across multiple locations. Renowned as world's leading university fostering entrepreneurship, we foster robust collaborations with academic, research, and industry stakeholders, both domestically and internationally.
