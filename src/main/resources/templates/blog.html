Our Certificate & Online Programs

At Austin University, we offer a diverse array of Certificate and Diploma programs tailored to suit individuals from diverse backgrounds and educational levels. Regardless of your starting point, our pathways allow for seamless progression into various academic disciplines. Whether you're aiming to enhance your career prospects with a university degree or simply seeking to broaden your understanding of the world, we have the perfect course to spark your curiosity!


At AUT, we prioritize the individual student experience. In our postgraduate studies, you'll benefit from intimate class sizes and personalized mentorship from experienced senior lecturers and renowned global researchers. Our Taught Masters Programs aim to cultivate and enrich students' expertise and competencies in their chosen subject area at the postgraduate level. These programs provide students with the chance to specialize in an area of interest that closely aligns with their undergraduate background or current professional trajectory.


To facilitate career advancement and exploration of new professional pathways, we provide a diverse selection of taught master's qualifications, such as Master of Arts, Master of Science, Master of Social Science, LLM, Master of Education, and Professional Master of Education. Additionally, at AUT University, we present Conversion Courses, allowing individuals to transition into a different field of study through specialized conversion Master's programs.


In 2024, AUT University provides level 8 Bachelor degree holders (or equivalent) with accessible pathways to delve into new fields of expertise through our popular conversion programs. These initiatives require no prior experience, offering a seamless transition into a new domain. Renowned for our excellent teaching, delivered by esteemed international researchers deeply engaged with their students, AUT fosters a vibrant campus community characterized by a student-centered and collaborative spirit. These attributes collectively shape a distinctive AUT student experience. We prioritize equipping graduates with essential skills in critical thinking and effective communication, recognizing these as paramount upon completing their university journey.
